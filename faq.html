Questions Every Investor Should Ask


1. Do you design and build from scratch to finish?
Answer:Yes
2. Can you build/develop outside your State? Yes
Answer:Yes
3. How flexible is your payment plan? Subject to negotiation
4. What is your fee and is it negotiable? Everything is negotiable and it doesn’t hurt to ask
5. Can you manage projects by other developers?
Answer:Yes
6. Type of Investment? Is it a short term or a long term investment?
7. Purpose of the Investment? Residential or Commercial?
8. The site of the investment: Will it serve its purpose or not?
9. Are there government policies that will affect the situation of the project in that area?
10. What are the types of land documentation or title of the property in view?
11. What is the actual cost of funding the project?
12. What is the cost of the investment as against its actual market value?
13. What are the likely returns on the investment?
14. What is the viability of the investment after a decade?
15. Are there any risks involved in the investment?
