Real Estates

In real estates, we offer the following services:
• Helping prospective tenants locate their ideal homes.
• Representing the buyer and helping the client find the best property, at the best price which meets the clients' need while protecting the client's interests.
• In representing a seller, we market the clients' property in an attempt to find a suitable buyer and obtain the best price for the seller.

In house management, we offer the following services:
• Issuance of schedule of payment for clients' clarification.
• Ensuring adequate sanitary condition of the property.
• Offering free legal services.
• Ensuring that the property is not misused by tenants.
• Ensuring tenants' compliance to the terms and conditions of their tenancy.

Building Construction
In building construction, we offer our services in three stages:

Stage 1: Preconstruction
We help the clients in locating genuine properties; do proper documentation/registration of the property title. Surveying of the property, architectural and structural drawings and building approvals.

Stage 2: Construction
We carry out project execution for our numerous clients. Our photo gallery portrays more of it.

Stage 3: Post Construction
We are involved in finishing, furnishing, equipping and maintenance of our projects.
